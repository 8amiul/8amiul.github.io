Breaking the complexity of Rubick's Cube

2022-11-19

○ Rubick's Cube

Solving rubick's cube is a big thing for me. From last week I start feeling like that I have to know how that cube is being solved. I haven't solved that before. I just tried and failed. It didn't take more than 1 one hour just to understand the strategy and the set of move patterns of that cube. I just watched 3 videos on YouTube and Those were really helpful.

[+] This video helped me to understand the fundamental knowledge of that cube. After following this video I was able to solve first 2 layers. But I was too dumb to understand the last part of this video. So then I switched to another video.

[+] I then solve the rubick's cube for the first time by watching this video. But as it was my first time it was not that fast and smooth. Also Had to fix a lot of problems in it. This method was very slow. Moreover sometimes at some point it felt like I fell into a loop. It mostly occurs at the last moment when I have left some pieces to fix. If I would apply the algorithm then it was like returning back to the same position again and again like a loop. It used to take 5 minute to solve the cube. Then I thought that I need to fix this, the speed. Though this video was very helpful for absolute beginner like me.

[+] Finally it's high time we made our solving speed fast to faster. I watched this tutorial which was from J Perm. It's a great video. But when I've first seen his video I didn't understand anything. After going through first two videos, I was able to understand this video. After watching this video I achieved the ability to solve rubick's cube with in 3 minute. Don't judge me I guess it is not bad as I have learned it by only practicing just one day. We all know and we must agree that practice makes man perfect. So I'll gonna practice more that I can be more faster.

[+] UPDATED! Now my average solving time is 2 min.[27 Nov 2022]

So that's how I have broken the complexity of rubick's cube. Now it has become a simple thing for me to solve. I like to learn new stuff. Also I have intended to learn new topics and relearn those topics which I have previously learned.
